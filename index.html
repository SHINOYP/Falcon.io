RENGOKU
GYOJIRO

Kyojuro Rengoku (煉れん獄ごく 杏きょう寿じゅ郎ろう Rengoku Kyōjurō?) was a major supporting character of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba and the deuteragonist of the Mugen Train Arc. He was a Demon Slayer of the Demon Slayer Corps and the late Flame Hashira (炎えん柱ばしら En Bashira?).[1]
